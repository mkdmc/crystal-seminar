\begin{abstract}
In this seminar paper, we provide a thorough introduction to the Crystal programming language; starting with its history and the motivations behind its emergence, to design principles, unique features, and well-known projects that utilize this language. At the end, we discuss our Crystal implementation of the \textbf{grep} utility, demonstrating the distinct Crystal features that helped us with writing this program. \\
